					CONTROLS
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
JUMPER - Multiplayer
Player 1 Controls:

Jump: W
Move Left: A
Move Right: D
Shoot: Left Shift

Player 2 Controls:

Jump: Up Arrow
Move Left: Left Arrow
Move Right: Right Arrow
Shoot: Right Shift

General Controls:
Escape: Quit the game

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
JUMPER - Singleplayer

Player 1 Controls:

Jump: W
Move Left: A
Move Right: D
Shoot: Left Shift
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
